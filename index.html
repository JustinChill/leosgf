Gisele Bündchen (2000 – 2005)

One of DiCaprio’s most famous relationships was with supermodel Gisele Bündchen. They began dating in 2000 and were often seen together at red carpet events and charity functions. Their relationship lasted for five years, ending in 2005, but they remain on good terms. Gisele has spoken fondly of her time with DiCaprio, despite their split.

Bar Refaeli (2005 – 2011)

Following his relationship with Gisele, DiCaprio dated Israeli supermodel Bar Refaeli. Their on-and-off relationship spanned several years, with the couple often spotted together at various events. They officially ended their relationship in 2011, and Refaeli later stated that their breakup was amicable.

Blake Lively (2011)

DiCaprio had a brief romance with actress Blake Lively in 2011. Their relationship was short-lived but highly publicized, as both were at the peak of their careers. They were spotted together on several occasions, but the romance fizzled out after a few months.

Toni Garrn (2013 – 2014)

In 2013, DiCaprio began dating German model Toni Garrn. Their relationship lasted for about a year, during which they attended various events together. They eventually split in late 2014, but Garrn has often expressed respect for DiCaprio, acknowledging their time together.

Nina Agdal (2016 – 2017)

DiCaprio was linked to model Nina Agdal in 2016. The couple was seen together on vacation and at public events, but their relationship ended in 2017 after about a year of dating. Agdal has since moved on but has occasionally commented on her time with DiCaprio positively.

Camila Morrone (2017 – 2022)

Leonardo DiCaprio’s most recent long-term relationship was with model and actress Camila Morrone. They began dating in late 2017 and were often seen together at various events, including the Oscars. However, the couple parted ways in early 2022 after nearly five years together, leading to much speculation about DiCaprio’s dating patterns.

Current Relationship Status

As of 2024, Leonardo DiCaprio is reportedly single, focusing on his career and environmental activism. He has been linked to various women since his split from Morrone, but no official relationships have been confirmed.
